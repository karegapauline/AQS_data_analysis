## The Kenya Environmental Management and Coordination (Air Quality) Regulations - A Breakdown
#### By Pauline Karega

There has been a significant change in the air quality space over the past decade. In terms of regulations, 2014 marked a key milestone, with the establishment of EMAC regulations 2014. 
And recent advances have resulted in an updated version of the act, the Environmental Management and Co-ordination (Air Quality) Regulations, 2024. 

For Kenya, a country grappling with growing urbanisation, expanding transport, industrial growth, and household air-pollution risks, this regulation offers a much-needed legal backbone to protect the air we breathe.
